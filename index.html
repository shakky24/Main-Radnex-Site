What Is Radnex

Radnex Interventional Science

Radnex Interventional Science is a Bangalore-based center dedicated to Interventional Radiology, a super-specialty that uses imaging technology to diagnose and treat conditions with high precision.

Instead of traditional approaches that rely on large incisions or generalized treatment, interventional radiology allows us to:

  • Target specific areas inside the body
  • Treat conditions through image-guided access
  • Improve outcomes with controlled, precise techniques

This approach is used across multiple specialties including gynecology, vascular conditions, thyroid, and breast care.

Precision-Led Medicine

Treatment guided by imaging, not guesswork.

Every procedure is planned and performed using real-time imaging such as ultrasound, fluoroscopy, or MRI, allowing for:

  • Accurate targeting of disease
  • Reduced impact on surrounding tissue
  • Controlled and effective treatment delivery

The goal is not just less invasive care, but better, more precise medicine.

When Should You Seek Evaluation?

Early evaluation creates more precise treatment options.

  • Persistent or unexplained pain
  • Abnormal bleeding
  • Noticeable lumps or swelling
  • Vascular discomfort or visible vein changes
  • Thyroid-related symptoms
